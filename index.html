Understanding HPV

HPV stands for Human Papillomavirus (인유두종바이러스). There are over 200 types of HPV.

Key Facts

  • Most common sexually transmitted infection in the U.S.
  • About 80% of people will get HPV at some point
  • Most people never know they have it
  • Body's immune system usually clears it within 2 years

Types of HPV

Low-Risk HPV

저위험 HPV

  • Cause genital warts
  • Not dangerous but uncomfortable

High-Risk HPV

고위험 HPV

  • Can cause cancer
  • Types 16 & 18 cause 70% of cervical cancers

HPV Vaccination Guide

Who Should Get Vaccinated?

Ages 11-12

권장 연령

Best time for vaccination - 2 doses needed

Ages 13-26

Catch-up vaccination - 3 doses

Is It Safe?

  • Given to over 135 million people worldwide
  • Continuously monitored by CDC and FDA
  • Side effects are typically mild

Does It Cause Infertility?

NO. This is a myth. Multiple studies confirm the vaccine does NOT affect fertility.

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: What is HPV?
A: HPV (Human Papillomavirus) is a very common virus that can cause genital warts and several types of cancer.
Q: Is the HPV vaccine safe?
A: Yes, the HPV vaccine is very safe. It has been studied extensively for over 15 years and given to over 135 million people worldwide.

한국어 자주 묻는 질문

Q: HPV가 무엇인가요?
A: HPV(인유두종바이러스)는 매우 흔한 바이러스로 성기 사마귀와 여러 종류의 암을 유발할 수 있습니다.
Q: HPV 백신은 안전한가요?
A: 네, HPV 백신은 매우 안전합니다. 15년 이상 광범위하게 연구되었으며 전 세계적으로 1억 3,500만 명 이상에게 투여되었습니다.
